Explore patterns and trends in total migration from a specific state in Australia according to 3 categories: Skilled, Family and Humanitarian, is the New South Wales state

How to use: You can hover over the dots in the state chart below and a box will appear containing text with the names of major cities in the state of New South Wales. In the line chart below, you can hover over the line, and it will display data for the 3 groups of people in the 2 areas above.

Explain

In showcasing the migration numbers for the three target groups across the states of Australia, our team has identified New South Wales as having the highest volume. Therefore, on this page, we have chosen to display New South Wales prominently to help users better understand this fact. Within the Greater Sydney region, notable cities include Sydney, Parramatta, and Central Coast. Transitioning to the rest of New South Wales, cities such as Newcastle, Wollongong, Tweed Heads, Maitland, Wagga Wagga, Albury, Coffs Harbour, Tamworth stand out. These cities are represented by red dots, symbolizing major urban centers within the state, with hover-over functionality to display city names. Furthermore, a line chart is provided to clearly illustrate the disparity in migration numbers between the two regions in a concise and coherent manner."
