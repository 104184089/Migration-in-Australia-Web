Excutive Summary

The main topic that our group will address in this project is migration, with thorough research and careful search for relevant data on the topic through a Table of Contents to show the information and prove the reason why our team chose the topic above.

To provide readers with a more comprehensive understanding of migration, we have depicted this through the Migration Data in Australia map below When users hover over states, a box contains information such as state names and migrant groups as well as the total number of migrants our team studied. Additionally, with dots containing the names of cities in the state, our team has also developed a similar feature to display state information. Furthermore, when the user clicks on the dots, a line graph for each city appears, illustrating the number of migrants for each group as mentioned above. Along with the appearance of the chart is the appearance of a bar chart providing information about the migration age milestones of each group of migrants, showing which age groups and types of migrants tend to migrate to Australia the most. To further prove our chosen point, our team conducted an analysis of one state, New South Wales, which has the highest number of migrants. Presented before the user is a detailed map of the state's territory, with dots representing several major cities, and hovering over them displays the city names. And right below is a stacked column chart of 3 object names for 2 areas considered the most developed in the state. Information and data will be displayed when the user hovers over the column.

In summary, regarding our website, we aim for users to have a fresh and comprehensive understanding of the migration topic, using Australia as an example. Our group has also provided various datasets to somewhat convey to users the general topic and the specific issue of migration to Australia.
