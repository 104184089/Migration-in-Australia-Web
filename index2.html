Explore the patterns and trends of migration classified by educational attainment in Australia

How to use: With this line chart, simply hovering over any of the three lines will display a text box containing the migrant type and the educational attainment levels associated with that migrant type.

Explain

The reason I want users to see this table is to provide them with a clearer understanding of the three migrant groups when they arrive in Australia. Particularly, it allows insight into the education and educational attainment levels of migrants within these groups upon their arrival in Australia. Consequently, users can grasp to some extent the reasons why migrants in these groups choose Australia as their destination.
